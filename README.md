## Aggregators & Databases

- [World Bank Open Data](https://data.worldbank.org/) — Largest open aggregator. Metrics: GDP, inflation, poverty, population, education, healthcare, energy, trade, employment (~1,400 indicators).
- [UN Data](https://data.un.org/) — UN data portal. Metrics: demography, crime, trade, production, gender, education, energy.
- [OECD Data](https://data.oecd.org/) — OECD countries + partners. Metrics: economy, innovation, taxes, health, migration, inequality, productivity.
- [Our World in Data](https://ourworldindata.org/) — Visualizations + datasets. Metrics: health, climate, poverty, war, nutrition, technology (thousands of datasets).
- [Gapminder](https://www.gapminder.org/data/) — Historical series from 19th century. Metrics: income, life expectancy, child mortality, population.
- [Statista](https://www.statista.com/) — Paid aggregator. Metrics: markets, consumption, industries, surveys (80k+ sources).
- [Trading Economics](https://tradingeconomics.com/) — Real-time macroeconomics. Metrics: GDP, inflation, interest rates, labor markets, stock markets, bonds.
- [Knoema](https://knoema.com/) — Dataset aggregator. Metrics: economy, industries, regional statistics.
- [CEIC Data](https://www.ceicdata.com/) — Paid macrodata. Metrics: 200+ countries, high-frequency indicators.
- [IMF Data](https://www.imf.org/en/Data) — IMF portal. Metrics: WEO forecasts, balance of payments, government debt, reserves, exchange rates.
- [Eurostat](https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat) — EU statistics. Metrics: economy, social statistics, agriculture, transport, environment across EU.
- [CIA World Factbook](https://www.cia.gov/the-world-factbook/) — Country profiles. Metrics: geography, demography, economy, government, military, communications.
- [Nationmaster](https://www.nationmaster.com/) — Country comparison across thousands of indicators.
- [Wikipedia: Lists by country](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lists_of_countries_and_territories) — Aggregated lists.
- [Indexmundi](https://www.indexmundi.com/) — Charts and rankings across many indicators.
- [Open Data Inventory (ODIN)](https://odin.opendatawatch.com/) — National statistical system quality and openness (~180 countries). Metrics: data coverage (breadth of topics), data openness (machine-readability, licensing, bulk download) across 22 categories.
- [UNCTADStat](https://unctadstat.unctad.org/) — UNCTAD statistical hub with country profiles. Metrics: merchandise trade, FDI flows, maritime transport connectivity (LSCI), commodity dependence, e-commerce readiness, development indicators. Distinct from World Bank/IMF in development-trade focus.
- [EBRD Transition Report](https://www.ebrd.com/home/news-and-events/publications/economics/transition-reports/) — Annual structural reform and macro data for 38 transition economies (Eastern Europe, Central Asia, MENA). Metrics: reform progress scores, green economy transition, Knowledge Economy Index (38 indicators across 4 innovation pillars).
- [IDB Open Data Portal](https://data.iadb.org/) — Inter-American Development Bank data for 26 Latin American and Caribbean countries (1,700+ indicators, series from 1940). Metrics: macro (Latin Macro Watch), social indicators, agricultural policy (Agrimonitor), trade integration (INTrade).

## Human Development & Quality of Life

- [Human Development Index (UNDP)](https://hdr.undp.org/data-center/human-development-index) — HDI. Metrics: life expectancy, education, GNI per capita. Also IHDI (inequality-adjusted), GDI, GII, MPI.
- [Legatum Prosperity Index](https://www.prosperity.com/) — 12 pillars of prosperity. Metrics: safety, freedom, governance, social capital, investment environment, health, education.
- [Social Progress Index](https://www.socialprogress.org/) — GDP-independent measure. Metrics: basic human needs, foundations of wellbeing, opportunity.
- [Quality of Life Index (Numbeo)](https://www.numbeo.com/quality-of-life/rankings_by_country.jsp) — Crowdsourced. Metrics: purchasing power, safety, healthcare, climate, traffic, pollution.
- [Mercer Quality of Living](https://www.mercer.com/insights/total-rewards/talent-mobility-insights/quality-of-living-city-ranking/) — City-level. Metrics: political environment, social environment, economy, healthcare, education, transport.
- [Better Life Index (OECD)](https://www.oecdbetterlifeindex.org/) — 11 topics. Metrics: housing, income, jobs, education, health, environment, work-life balance.
- [Where-to-be-born Index (EIU)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Where-to-be-born_Index) — Forecasted quality of life at birth.
- [Good Country Index](https://index.goodcountry.org/) — How much each country contributes to global good. Metrics: science & technology, culture, international peace & security, world order, planet & climate, prosperity & equality, health & wellbeing.
- [U.S. News Best Countries](https://www.usnews.com/news/best-countries/rankings) — Perception-based ranking via 17k+ global citizens. Sub-rankings: adventure, agility, cultural influence, entrepreneurship, heritage, power, quality of life, social purpose, open for business.
- [SDG Index (SDSN)](https://dashboards.sdgindex.org/) — Progress toward all 17 UN Sustainable Development Goals. 167 countries scored 0–100. Includes per-SDG dashboards and trend arrows.
- [InterNations Expat Insider](https://www.internations.org/expat-insider/) — Annual survey of 10k+ expats. Metrics: working abroad, personal finance, quality of life, expat essentials, ease of settling in.
- [World Bank Human Capital Index (HCI)](https://www.worldbank.org/en/publication/human-capital) — Human capital a child born today can expect to accumulate by age 18. Metrics: survival to age 5, expected school years, harmonized test scores, adult survival rate, stunting rate (0–1 composite, 174 countries).
- [EIU Global Liveability Index](https://www.eiu.com/n/campaigns/global-liveability-index/) — Annual city liveability ranking (173 cities). Metrics: stability, healthcare, culture & environment, education, infrastructure. 0–100 score, annual series since 2002.
- [IMD Smart City Index](https://www.imd.org/smart-city-observatory/home/) — Resident-perception smart city ranking (142+ cities, 120 surveys per city). Metrics: structures (physical infrastructure) and technology (digital services) across health/safety, mobility, economic opportunities, governance digitalization.
- [Global Power City Index (GPCI)](https://mori-m-foundation.or.jp/english/ius2/gpci2/index.shtml) — City magnetism for people, capital, enterprises (48 major cities). Metrics: economy, R&D, cultural interaction, livability, environment, accessibility. Annual, Mori Memorial Foundation.
- [Demographia International Housing Affordability](http://www.demographia.com/dhi.pdf) — Housing affordability in 95 major markets across 8 nations (Australia, Canada, China, Ireland, NZ, Singapore, UK, USA). Metrics: median multiple (house price / household income), affordability bands (affordable ≤3.0 → impossibly unaffordable ≥9.0).
- [Bertelsmann Social Cohesion Radar](https://www.socialcohesion.info/) — Social cohesion diagnostic (34 OECD countries + 22-country Asian edition, trends since 2013). Metrics: 9 dimensions across 3 domains — Social Relations (social networks, trust in people, acceptance of diversity), Connectedness (identification, institutional trust, perceived fairness), Focus on Common Good (solidarity, rule observance, civic participation). Designed as democratic resilience diagnostic; global expansion forthcoming.
- [OECD Society at a Glance](https://www.oecd.org/en/publications/society-at-a-glance_19991290-en.html) — Social policy indicators (~50 OECD + key partner countries, biennial). Metrics: 25 indicators across 5 chapters — General Context, Self-Sufficiency, Equity, Health, Social Cohesion — including social mobility, loneliness, benefit coverage, homicide rates, subjective wellbeing. Distinct from OECD Better Life Index (interactive, user-weighted, 11 topics) in providing the underlying statistical digest with full time-series and additional equity/cohesion dimensions.

## Economy & Finance

- [IMF World Economic Outlook](https://www.imf.org/en/Publications/WEO) — GDP, inflation, unemployment, government debt, current account, forecasts.
- [World Bank World Development Indicators](https://databank.worldbank.org/source/world-development-indicators) — WDI. Metrics: economy, poverty, trade, FDI.
- [Doing Business (archive)](https://archive.doingbusiness.org/) — Closed 2021. Metrics: business registration, permits, taxes, cross-border trade, enforcing contracts.
- [Business Ready (B-READY, World Bank)](https://www.worldbank.org/en/businessready) — Doing Business successor. Metrics: regulatory environment, public services, operational efficiency.
- [Global Competitiveness Index (WEF, archive)](https://www.weforum.org/reports/the-global-competitiveness-report-2020) — 12 pillars of competitiveness.
- [IMD World Competitiveness Ranking](https://www.imd.org/centers/wcc/world-competitiveness-center/rankings/world-competitiveness-ranking/) — Economic performance, government efficiency, business efficiency, infrastructure.
- [Heritage Economic Freedom Index](https://www.heritage.org/index/) — Economic freedom. Metrics: rule of law, government size, regulatory efficiency, open markets.
- [Fraser Economic Freedom of the World](https://www.fraserinstitute.org/economic-freedom) — Similar to Heritage. 5 areas.
- [Gini Index (World Bank)](https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/SI.POV.GINI) — Income inequality.
- [World Inequality Database](https://wid.world/) — Income/wealth distribution, top 1%, top 10%.
- [UBS Global Wealth Report](https://www.ubs.com/global/en/family-office-uhnw/reports/global-wealth-report.html) — Household wealth, millionaires, ultra-high-net-worth.
- [Big Mac Index (Economist)](https://www.economist.com/big-mac-index) — PPP via burger price.
- [Numbeo Cost of Living](https://www.numbeo.com/cost-of-living/) — Prices, rent, groceries, transport by city.
- [Expatistan](https://www.expatistan.com/cost-of-living) — City cost-of-living comparison.
- [Forbes Global 2000](https://www.forbes.com/lists/global2000/) — Largest public companies.
- [Fortune Global 500](https://fortune.com/ranking/global500/) — Largest by revenue.
- [S&P Global Ratings / Moody's / Fitch](https://www.spglobal.com/ratings/en/) — Sovereign credit ratings.
- [Global Debt Monitor (IIF)](https://www.iif.com/Products/Global-Debt-Monitor) — Government, corporate, and household debt.
- [Kearney FDI Confidence Index](https://www.kearney.com/service/global-business-policy-council/foreign-direct-investment-confidence-index) — Annual survey of 500+ executives. Ranks markets likely to attract most FDI in next 3 years.
- [Natixis Global Retirement Index](https://www.im.natixis.com/en-us/insights/investor-sentiment/2025/global-retirement-index) — 44 developed countries. Metrics: health, quality of life, material wellbeing, finances in retirement.
- [Mercer CFA Institute Global Pension Index](https://www.mercer.com/insights/investments/market-outlook-and-trends/mercer-cfa-global-pension-index/) — Pension system design quality (48 systems, 65% of world population). Metrics: adequacy of benefit levels, sustainability given demographics, integrity of regulation and governance. A–E grade.
- [Global Findex Database (World Bank)](https://www.worldbank.org/en/publication/globalfindex) — Financial inclusion demand-side survey (141 economies, ~150k adults). Metrics: account ownership, digital payments, savings, borrowing, mobile money by income quintile and gender.
- [IMF Financial Development Index](https://data.imf.org/?sk=f8032e80-b36c-43b1-ac26-493c5b1cd33b) — Financial system depth (180+ countries). Metrics: financial institutions and markets depth, access, efficiency — composite and sub-index scores.
- [Atlas of Economic Complexity (Harvard Growth Lab)](https://atlas.hks.harvard.edu/) — Economic Complexity Index (ECI). Metrics: country ECI ranking, product space diversity, 10-year GDP per capita growth forecasts by country.
- [International Property Rights Index (IPRI)](https://internationalpropertyrightsindex.org/) — Property rights protection (125 countries). Metrics: legal & political environment, physical property rights (registration, credit access), intellectual property rights (patents, copyright, trademarks).
- [US Chamber GIPC International IP Index](https://www.uschamber.com/intellectual-property/2026-international-ip-index) — IP law strength (55 economies, annual since 2012). Metrics: 53 criteria across 8 categories — Patents, Copyrights, Trademarks, Trade Secrets, Commercialization, Enforcement, Systemic Efficiency, International Treaty membership. Used in bilateral trade negotiations and pharma/tech market-entry risk; distinct from IPRI (broader property rights) and GII WIPO (innovation outputs).
- [Sovereign Wealth Fund Institute (SWFI)](https://www.swfinstitute.org/fund-rankings/sovereign-wealth-fund) — SWF rankings. Metrics: fund AUM, country of origin, asset allocation, Linaburg-Maduell Transparency Index.
- [PRS Group International Country Risk Guide (ICRG)](https://www.prsgroup.com/explore-our-products/icrg/) — Country risk ratings since 1980 (141 countries, monthly). Metrics: political risk (12 components: government stability, conflict, corruption, law & order, democratic accountability), economic risk, financial risk.
- [Coface Country Risk Assessment](https://www.coface.com/news-economy-and-insights/business-risk-dashboard/country-risk-map) — Country and trade credit risk (160 countries, quarterly). Metrics: country risk rating (A1–D), business climate assessment, sector risk overlays.
- [Allianz Trade Country Risk Atlas](https://www.allianz-trade.com/en_global/news-insights/news/allianz-trade-country-risk-atlas.html) — Trade credit and non-payment risk (84+ countries, quarterly). Metrics: country grade (AA–D), risk level (1–4), financial flows and cyclical risk indicators.
- [OECD Country Risk Classification](https://www.oecd.org/en/topics/country-risk-classification.html) — Official export credit risk classification used by OECD ECAs (150+ countries). Metrics: risk category 0–7 (transfer/convertibility + force majeure risk). Basis for minimum export credit premium rates globally.
- [OECD Consumer & Business Confidence Indices](https://data.oecd.org/leadind/consumer-confidence-index-cci.htm) — Monthly sentiment (40+ economies). Metrics: Consumer Confidence Index (CCI) and Business Confidence Index (BCI), both normalised to 100 baseline; composite leading indicator (CLI) with turning-point signals.
- [OECD TiVA (Trade in Value Added)](https://oe.cd/tiva) — Global value chain analysis (76 economies, 45 industries, 1995–2020). Metrics: domestic vs. foreign value added in exports, GVC participation index, backward/forward integration, bilateral value-added trade flows.
- [Z/Yen Global Financial Centres Index (GFCI)](https://www.longfinance.net/programmes/financial-centre-futures/global-financial-centres-index/) — Financial centre competitiveness (121 centres, twice yearly). Metrics: business environment, human capital, infrastructure, financial sector development, fintech, green finance.
- [UNEP Inclusive Wealth Index](https://www.unep.org/resources/report/inclusive-wealth-report) — Beyond-GDP wealth measure (163 countries, 1990–2019). Metrics: produced capital, human capital, natural capital per capita; annual growth rates; natural capital depletion risk.
- [Geopolitical Risk Index (Caldara & Iacoviello)](https://www.matteoiacoviello.com/gpr.htm) — Text-based geopolitical threat index (monthly, 44 country-specific series, back to 1900). Metrics: GPR Threat sub-index, GPR Act sub-index; 8 categories: war threats/acts, nuclear threats, terror threats/acts, military buildups.
- [Commitment to Development Index (CGD)](https://www.cgdev.org/cdi) — Rich-country policy impact on developing world (40 countries). Metrics: finance (aid quality, tax haven behaviour), technology, environment (GHG, fisheries subsidies), trade tariffs, security, migration, health, investment.
- [EY Global Shadow Economy Report](https://www.ey.com/en_gl/insights/tax/why-the-shadow-economy-persists-and-how-governments-are-responding) — Shadow/informal economy size (131 countries, 2000–2023). Metrics: shadow economy as % of GDP and absolute USD, derived via currency demand approach tracking cash use patterns. Distinct from Gini/WID which measure formal inequality; enables analysis of tax capacity erosion and informal labour conditions.
- [OECD FDI Regulatory Restrictiveness Index](https://www.oecd.org/en/topics/sub-issues/sustainable-investment/fdi-regulatory-restrictiveness-index.html) — Statutory barriers to foreign direct investment (100+ countries, 22 sectors, biennial). Metrics: 0–1 scale across four restriction types — foreign equity limits, screening/approval requirements, key personnel restrictions, operational restrictions. Purely law-based (no surveys); standard tool for trade negotiators. Distinct from Kearney FDI Confidence Index which surveys executive intentions.
- [SolAbility Global Sustainable Competitiveness Index (GSCI)](https://solability.com/the-global-sustainable-competitiveness-index) — Competitiveness via sustainable wealth creation (192 countries, annual since 2012). Metrics: 216 hard indicators (no surveys) across 6 sub-indices — Natural Capital, Resource Efficiency, Social Capital, Intellectual Capital & Innovation, Economic Sustainability, Governance Capital. Explicitly substitutes inclusive wealth creation for GDP as primary competitiveness metric.
- [Henley Private Wealth Migration Report](https://www.henleyglobal.com/publications/henley-private-wealth-migration-report-2025) — Annual cross-border flows of high-net-worth individuals (>$1M liquid assets, ~100 countries). Metrics: net millionaire inflows/outflows per country, fastest-growing wealth markets. New World Wealth proprietary database of 150k+ HNWIs cross-referenced against property registers and company filings. Distinct from UBS Global Wealth Report which measures stock of wealth.
- [Swiss Re sigma](https://sigma.swissre.com/) — Global insurance market data (86+ countries, annual since 1968). Metrics: total premiums, insurance penetration (% of GDP), insurance density (per capita) for life and non-life segments. Only authoritative global insurance dataset; used by IMF, World Bank, FSB for financial sector benchmarking. Proxy for financial system depth and societal risk absorption capacity.
- [MSCI Market Classification](https://www.msci.com/indexes/index-resources/market-classification) — Annual equity market development status (80+ markets: 23 Developed, 24 Emerging, 32 Frontier, Standalone). Metrics: economic development, market size/liquidity, market accessibility for foreign investors; annual review with promotions/demotions/watchlist. Drives trillions in passive investment flows; most consequential single country capital-markets designation globally.
- [Milken Institute Global Opportunity Index (GOI)](https://milkeninstitute.org/global-opportunity-index) — Investment attractiveness of emerging/developing economies (115 countries, annual). Metrics: 101 variables across 5 pillars — Business Perception, Economic Fundamentals, Financial Services, Institutional Framework, International Standards & Policy. Objective data only; distinct from Kearney FDI Confidence (executive surveys) and focused exclusively on non-OECD markets.
- [Global Quality Infrastructure Index (GQII)](https://gqii.org/) — National quality infrastructure (185 countries, 2023 edition). Metrics: Metrology (calibration labs, BIPM membership), Standardization (ISO output, national standards body), Accreditation (conformity assessment bodies). Developed by PTB (Germany's metrology institute) with Mesopartner and TU Berlin. Prerequisite for export market access; not covered by any listed competitiveness or trade index.

- [UN DESA Population Division](https://www.un.org/development/desa/pd/) — Urbanization, migration, aging.
- [Worldometer](https://www.worldometers.info/) — Real-time population, growth rates.

- [US Census International Database](https://www.census.gov/data-tools/demo/idb/) — Demographics for 227 countries.

- [DHS Program](https://dhsprogram.com/) — Health and demographic surveys for developing countries.

## Democracy, Freedom & Human Rights

- [Freedom House — Freedom in the World](https://freedomhouse.org/report/freedom-world) — Political rights (1–40), civil liberties (1–60). Categories: Free / Partly Free / Not Free.
- [Freedom House — Freedom on the Net](https://freedomhouse.org/report/freedom-net) — Internet freedom.
- [Freedom House — Nations in Transit](https://freedomhouse.org/report/nations-transit) — Post-communist countries.
- [EIU Democracy Index](https://www.eiu.com/n/campaigns/democracy-index/) — Full/flawed democracy, hybrid, authoritarian. 60 indicators.
- [V-Dem (Varieties of Democracy)](https://v-dem.net/) — 500+ democracy indicators since 1789. Metrics: electoral, liberal, participatory, deliberative, egalitarian.
- [Polity Project](https://www.systemicpeace.org/polityproject.html) — Autocracy/democracy score –10 to +10.
- [Bertelsmann Transformation Index (BTI)](https://bti-project.org/) — Political and economic transformation.
- [Human Freedom Index (Cato/Fraser)](https://www.cato.org/human-freedom-index) — Personal + economic freedoms.
- [CIVICUS Monitor](https://monitor.civicus.org/) — Civil society freedom (open/narrowed/obstructed/repressed/closed).
- [Amnesty International Reports](https://www.amnesty.org/en/countries/) — Human rights country profiles.
- [Human Rights Watch World Report](https://www.hrw.org/world-report) — Human rights violations.
- [US State Department Human Rights Reports](https://www.state.gov/reports-bureau-of-democracy-human-rights-and-labor/country-reports-on-human-rights-practices/) — Annual country reports.
- [Rule of Law Index (WJP)](https://worldjusticeproject.org/rule-of-law-index/) — 8 factors: constraints on government, absence of corruption, open government, fundamental rights, order, civil and criminal justice.
- [Global Slavery Index (Walk Free)](https://www.walkfree.org/global-slavery-index/) — Modern slavery (160 countries). Metrics: prevalence per 1,000 people, total numbers, government response score (A–F), vulnerability factors (governance, social protection, inequality, conflict).
- [ILGA-Europe Rainbow Map](https://rainbowmap.ilga-europe.org/) — LGBTQ+ legal equality in 49 European countries (annual). 0–100 composite score across 75 criteria: equality & non-discrimination, family rights, hate crime/speech laws, legal gender recognition, intersex bodily integrity, civil society space, asylum.
- [ILGA World — State-Sponsored Homophobia](https://ilga.org/state-sponsored-homophobia-report/) — Legal status of LGBTQ+ people globally (193 UN member states, annual). Metrics: criminalization status, penalty severity (including death penalty risk), conversion therapy bans, legal gender recognition, same-sex partnership/marriage recognition, anti-discrimination protections.
- [Political Terror Scale (PTS)](https://www.politicalterrorscale.org/) — State-perpetrated political violence (180+ countries, annual since 1976). 1–5 scale measuring scope and intensity of political imprisonment, torture, extrajudicial killings, disappearances. Coded independently from Amnesty International, Human Rights Watch, and US State Department — three separate annual scores per country enable triangulation.
- [International IDEA Global State of Democracy Indices (GSoD)](https://www.idea.int/democracytracker/gsod-indices) — 30 democracy sub-indices (173 countries, 1975–present). Metrics: representation, rights, rule of law, participation — plus electoral integrity, civil liberties, judicial independence, media integrity. Fully open download.
- [Electoral Integrity Project — PEI Index](https://www.electoralintegrityproject.com/pei) — Expert-assessed quality of individual elections (480+ elections, 169 countries, Harvard/Sydney). Metrics: 49 indicators across 11 electoral cycle stages — laws, registration, redistricting, campaign finance, media, voting, count, results, authorities. 0–100 PEI score per election. Distinct from V-Dem and EIU Democracy Index in operating election-by-election, enabling within-country comparison across cycles.

## Corruption & Governance

- [Transparency International — Corruption Perceptions Index](https://www.transparency.org/en/cpi) — CPI, 0–100.
- [World Bank — Worldwide Governance Indicators](https://info.worldbank.org/governance/wgi/) — WGI. 6 dimensions: voice & accountability, political stability, government effectiveness, regulatory quality, rule of law, corruption control.
- [Global Corruption Barometer](https://www.transparency.org/en/gcb) — Citizen surveys on corruption experience.
- [Basel AML Index](https://index.baselgovernance.org/) — Money laundering risk.
- [Tax Justice Network — Financial Secrecy Index](https://fsi.taxjustice.net/) — Financial secrecy.
- [Open Budget Index](https://internationalbudget.org/open-budget-survey) — Budget transparency.
- [Ibrahim Index of African Governance (IIAG)](https://mo.ibrahim.foundation/iiag) — 54 African countries. 81 indicators across security & rule of law, participation & human rights, sustainable economic opportunity, human development.
- [FATF High-Risk Jurisdictions](https://www.fatf-gafi.org/en/countries/black-and-grey-lists.html) — AML/CFT compliance blacklist and grey list (updated 3× per year). Metrics: blacklist/grey-list status, strategic deficiencies identified, action plan compliance timeline.
- [PEFA (Public Expenditure and Financial Accountability)](https://www.pefa.org/assessments) — Public financial management quality (country assessment reports). Metrics: 31 indicators across budget credibility, transparency, execution, accounting/reporting, and external audit.
- [Government Defence Integrity Index (GDI)](https://ti-defence.org/gdi/) — Institutional corruption risk in defence and security sectors (~90 countries). Metrics: five risk pillars (financial, operational, personnel, political, procurement), 77 indicators. A–F risk band.
- [EITI Country Compliance](https://eiti.org/) — Extractive industries revenue transparency (55 member countries). Metrics: validation score (0–100), contract disclosure, beneficial ownership disclosure, revenue data completeness. Status: Compliant / Satisfactory / Inadequate / Suspended.
- [Resource Governance Index (NRGI)](https://resourcegovernanceindex.org/) — Governance quality of oil, gas, and mining sectors in resource-rich countries. Metrics: value realization (licensing, contracts, taxation), revenue management, enabling environment. 0–100 score.
- [Atlas of Impunity (Eurasia Group)](https://www.eurasiagroup.net/live-post/atlas-of-impunity-2024) — Multi-domain impunity measure (170 countries, 60 indicators). Metrics: unaccountable governance, human rights abuses, economic exploitation, conflict and violence, environmental degradation. 0–5 scale.
- [Aid Transparency Index (Publish What You Fund)](https://www.publishwhatyoufund.org/the-index/) — Transparency of the 50 largest development donors. Metrics: IATI data completeness, frequency, timeliness, interlinkage. 0–100 score, tier classification (very good → very poor).
- [TRACE Bribery Risk Matrix](https://www.traceinternational.org/trace-matrix) — Commercial bribery risk for businesses (194 countries). Metrics: opportunity (govt interaction), anti-bribery deterrence, transparency, oversight capacity. 0–100 score, distinct from CPI in B2G bribery focus.
- [Prindex — Global Property Rights Index](https://www.prindex.net/) — Perception-based land and housing tenure security (108 countries, ~5 billion people). Metrics: % adults feeling insecure in land/housing rights, disaggregated by gender/income/urban-rural, formal vs. informal tenure.
- [Bertelsmann SGI (Sustainable Governance Indicators)](https://www.sgi-network.org/) — Governance quality in 30 OECD/EU countries. Metrics: sustainable policymaking (economic, social, environmental outcomes), governing with foresight (strategic capacity, evidence-based decisions), democratic government (rule of law, civil rights, media freedom).
- [Chandler Good Government Index (CGGI)](https://www.chandlerinstitute.org/cggi) — Government operational capability (120 countries, annual since 2021). Metrics: 35 indicators across 7 pillars — leadership & foresight, robust laws & policies, strong institutions, financial stewardship, attractive marketplace, global influence, helping people rise. Uses Amnesty, HRW, US State Dept, and UN sources.

## Labor & Workers

- [ITUC Global Rights Index](https://www.ituc-csi.org/ituc-global-rights-index) — Workers' rights violations (148 countries, 1–5+ scale). - [ILOSTAT](https://ilostat.ilo.org/) — ILO primary statistical portal (300M+ data points). Metrics: employment rates, informality, wages, social protection coverage, labour productivity, gender employment gaps.
